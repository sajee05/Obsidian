#### LOK SABHA SPEAKER SHRI OM BIRLA TO VISIT MUSSOORIE, UTTARAKHAND TOMORROW

##### Lok Sabha Speaker's Visit Details
- **Lok Sabha Speaker Shri Om Birla** to visit **Mussoorie, Uttarakhand** tomorrow.
- He will address the **valedictory ceremony** of the **127th Induction Training Program**.
- Event to be held at **Lal Bahadur Shastri National Academy of Administration (LBSNAA)**.
- Speaker to arrive at **Jolly Grant Airport** at 9:45 AM on **12 June 2025**.
- He will reach Mussoorie at **11:30 AM**.
- The Lok Sabha Speaker is scheduled to **return to Delhi** the same day.

##### 127th Induction Training Program Details
- The program is designed for **officers promoted** from **State Civil Services** to **Indian Administrative Service**.
- It provides a structured platform for **transitioning roles**.
- Officers move from state-level to **national-level leadership responsibilities**.

##### Participants of the 127th Induction Training Program
- A total of **97 officers** are participating in this program.
- Officers are promoted from services of **19 states**.
- The program includes **73 male officers**.
- It also includes **24 female officers**.

##### Vision and Objectives of the Program
- The program is inspired by **“Viksit Bharat @2047”**.
- It aligns with the transformational goals of **“Mission Karmayogi”**.
- The objective is to nurture **ethical, capable, and future-ready civil servants**.
- Officers are equipped for **policy-making**.
- They are also trained for **inter-regional coordination**.
- The program prepares them for **institutional leadership**.

##### Key Thematic Pillars
- The program is based on **seven key thematic pillars**.
- These pillars include **Good Governance**.
- Another pillar is **Personality Development**.
- **Collaborative Learning** is also a key theme.
- **Technology** forms a crucial pillar.
- **Leadership Insights** are provided.
- **Entrepreneurship** is also covered.
- The final pillar is **Regional Understanding**.
