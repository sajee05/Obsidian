**Phrases:**

- 'In conclusion, it can be inferred that X...'

**Usage:**  
- 'Thus far, the evidence underscores *three gaps in MGNREGA's wage payments*'

- 'Summing up, it is evident that *women-led SHGs are pivotal for rural development*'
