## Front

Pteridophytes

<hr/>

## Back

Pteron (feather) + phytes (plants)  
  
→ Vascular plants that reproduce via spores, like ferns.  
  
Examples: Ferns, Horsetails  
  
Layman Explanation: These plants don’t have flowers or seeds; they reproduce using spores.
