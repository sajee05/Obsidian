## Front

Epiphytes

<hr/>

## Back

epi (upon) + phyton (plant)  
  
→ Plants that grow upon other plants.  
  
**Examples:** Orchids, Ferns, Spanish Moss  
  
**Layman Explanation:** These plants **sit on other trees** but don’t harm them, they just use them for support.
