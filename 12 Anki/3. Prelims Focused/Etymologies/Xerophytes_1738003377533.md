## Front

Xerophytes

<hr/>

## Back

xero (dry) + phyte (Plant)  
i.e. plant adapted to dry conditions  
  
**Examples:** Cactus, Aloe Vera, Agave  
**Layman Explanation:** These plants **store water** to survive in deserts where it hardly rains.
